🧭 Welcome to Your Finance Dashboard!

This short guide helps you understand each part of your dashboard and how to use it effectively.

📌 1. Summary Snapshot

• Month at a glance – expenses, savings, net cash flow, credits, CC outstanding
• Includes an Insights block highlighting key patterns.

💰 2. Budget Summary

• Shows overall budget and remaining amount
• Displays category-wise budgets, so you can see where you are over or under.

📈 3. Charts

• Category Pie → where your money goes
• Daily Trend → how expenses are distributed across days
• Card Spend vs Bills → usage and repayments for each card.

🔎 4. Search & Filter

• Search by notes, category, card, type, date range, and amount range.
• Results are shown in a detailed table.

⚡ 5. Quick Actions

• Time-based shortcuts (Last 7 days, This Month, etc.)
• Large spend filters (>1000, >3000, >5000)
• Category shortcuts (Food, Shopping, Investments, etc.).

📆 6. Daily Summary

• Shows total debit, credit, and net for each day.

➕ 7. Add Transaction

• Add entries as EXP, CC, BILL, CR, or SAV
• Use clear categories (Food, MF, NPS, GOLD, etc.) and notes.
